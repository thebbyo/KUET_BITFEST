Suggestion: How about preparing a simple plain cooked rice? Here's a simple recipe:

Ingredients:
- Rice: 2 cups

Steps:
1. Clean the rice under cold tap water until the water runs clear.
2. Transfer the rinsed rice into a pot and add 4 cups of water.
3. Cover the pot and turn the heat to high.
4. Once the water is boiling, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes or until the water has been completely absorbed by the rice.
5. Fluff the rice with a fork, and it's ready to serve!
